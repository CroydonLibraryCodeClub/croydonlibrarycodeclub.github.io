About Us

Code clubs are targeted at children between the ages of 9 and 13 and run by teachers and volunteers. Our club runs from 10.00am to 11.00am on Saturday mornings at Croydon Central library in the children ºs library section.

What happens at our club?

At club sessions, young people follow the project guides specially created by Code Club. Our organisers provide support to help young people progress through the projects, to create games, animations, and web pages using Scratch, Python, or HTML/CSS.
