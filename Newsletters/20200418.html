Welcome to the first Croydon Library Code Club weekly newsletter.

Each week, we'll be setting you coding challenges for you to complete at home, while we are all stuck in lockdown. Each challenge will be based on a theme. You will have until Thursday to submit your entries and we'll feature the best of your work in next week's newsletter.

Challenge

This week's theme is: Thank You Key Workers!

Make a game, picture, animation, story, poem, or song, to say thank you to all those people risking their lives every day to keep us safe during the COVID-19 lockdown. You can make your entry in Scratch, HTML, or Python.
