Cake room
Ingredients: -
  • 200g caster sugar.
  • 125g self-raising flour.
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 125ml milk.
  • 75g butter, melted.
Method: -
  1. Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas 4. Grease a cake tin.
  2. Put all dry ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  3. Add eggs, vanilla extract and milk, mix well. Add melted butter and stir for a few minutes.
  4. Pour batter into cake tin. Bake in preheated oven for about 45 minutes. The cake is done when a skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean.
