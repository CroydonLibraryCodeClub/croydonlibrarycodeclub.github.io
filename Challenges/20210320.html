Challenge 20/03/2021: New Beginnings

The 20th of March is World Storytelling Day and the theme this year is New Beginnings.

World Storytelling Day facts

  • World Storytelling Day started in Sweden around 1991
  • in Swedish it is called Alla berättares dag
  • the date is when we have an equal amount of day and night

Projects

Tell your story with our Tell a story HTML / CSS project.

Or follow the built in Scratch Code a Cartoon tutorial to illustrate your story

For your new beginning you could be the first to talk to an alien with our Chatbot project in Scratch.

Or make your own project in whatever platform you like. Send us your project and we will feature it in the newsletter.
