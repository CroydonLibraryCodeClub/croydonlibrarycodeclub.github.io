Challenge 26/09/2020: Pass the Torch

What does the theme Pass the Torch mean to you?

The torch relay was introduced in the 1936 Olympic Games in Berlin.

The phrase to pass the torch has come to mean passing on a tradition or vision.

Perhaps you could make a torch relay race by adapting our sprint project.

Or maybe make a web page showing how make one of your own projects.

Platform

Scratch Python HTML Unity

Backgrounds

Good places for races:

Beach Malibu Beach Rio Blue Sky Boardwalk Desert Metro Playground Playing Field

Sprites

Racing sprites:

Ben Jordyn Tatiana Taylor Trisha

There is no torch, you will have to paint your own!

Sound Effects

There are no torch sounds either, but you might record your own. Try crumpling paper or a plastic bag in front of your microphone to make a good fire sound!
