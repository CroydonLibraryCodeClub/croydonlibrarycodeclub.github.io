Challenge 09/05/2020: Recycling Bin Crafts

Make fun things out of stuff you find in your recycling bin!

  • What can you turn that cardboard box into?
  • Does that empty soap bottle look like a penguin?
  • Those bottle caps could be eyes! Or wheels! Or ??

With some basic materials you can find around your home (scissors, tape, glue), make something fun from stuff in your recycle bin and show how you made it in a Scratch project!

Thanks to Scratch Team members @dinopickles and @BrycedTea for creating this activity!
