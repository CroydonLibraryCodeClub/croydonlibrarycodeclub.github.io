Challenge 19/09/2020: Glide

Make a project inspired by the theme: glide.

Getting Started

What does the theme glide mean to you? Maybe you could use the glide block to animate a sprite. How about a gliding game?

Lost in space

Our Lost in space project uses the glide block to animate a space ship.

Dodgeball

Our Dodgeball game project uses the glide block to animate the ball obstacles.

Binary hero

If you are an advanced Scratch programmer, you could try our Binary hero project, which uses the glide block to move the notes in the game.
