Challenges 23/05/2020

Boredom Busters

Projects that make life easier… or more fun… or more weird.

Instructions | Instrucciones | Istruzioni | Instruções | 指示 | Talimatlar | Navodila

Bored at home? Add randomness to an activity you do at home! This studio is dedicated to making your everyday life at home a little more interesting.

Here are some ideas of what you can do:

  • Make a virtual gym class instructor who gives you workout moves
  • Create a game that teaches you ridiculous dance moves
  • Make a fortune telling project or a magic 8 ball
  • Make a mindfulness project to help you relax or sleep
  • Take a photo of everyday objects and doodle on them in the paint editor
  • Create your own spinner for a game like Twister
  • Create your own Bored at Home Bingo Board
  • Add sound effects to your everyday life by making a soundboard

Hope these ideas add some joy to your day. We can’t wait to see what you make!

Thanks to Scratch Team members @kittyloaf and @pondermake for creating this prompt.
