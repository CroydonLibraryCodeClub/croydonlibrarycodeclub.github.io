Challenge 29/05/2021: Clones

Clones are exact copies. We use clones in Scratch to make copies of sprites.

Clone facts

The first mammal cloned was Dolly the sheep. She was born on the 5th of July, 1996.

Projects

You could try our clone wars Scratch game project.

Or lead the cat clones to safety in our CATS! Scratch game project.

If you prefer to use Python, you can race turtle clones in our Turtle Race! Python project.
