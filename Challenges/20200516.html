Challenge 16/05/2020: How Do You Peace Out?

Design projects that help you and others feel calmer.

Instructions | Instrucciones | Istruzioni | Instruções | 指示 | Talimatlar | Navodila

Everyone has different things that make them feel more calm. For some people it’s a special memory, for others it could be certain sounds or images. What makes you feel most peaceful? Tell us about it in your project notes.

Here are some ideas to think about:

  • Animate a pattern of shapes that move slowly around the screen
  • Include peaceful music or sounds in your project
  • Create a story that shares a special peaceful memory

These are just ideas: please feel free to add your own ideas as well!

Thanks to Scratch Team members @pixelmoth, @lilyland, and @chrisg for creating this prompt.
