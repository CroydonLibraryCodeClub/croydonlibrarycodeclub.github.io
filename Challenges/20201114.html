Challenge 14/11/2020: Tennis

What kind of project will you make for the theme of Tennis?

Did you know?

One of the first computer games ever made was Tennis for Two:

The game of Tennis is over 100 years old and was first played in the 1870's.

If you win a point when you serve and your opponent did not touch the ball it is called an ace.

Projects

Or can you create a score board in Python that counts up the game points and set points like the real tennis score boards?
