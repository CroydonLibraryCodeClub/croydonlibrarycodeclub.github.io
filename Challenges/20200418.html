Challenge 18/04/2020: Thank You Key Workers!

Make a game, picture, animation, story, poem, or song, to say thank you to all those people risking their lives every day to keep us safe during the COVID-19 lockdown. You can make your entry in Scratch, HTML, or Python.

Getting Started

If you are working in Scratch, you can click this link to remix a starter project.

If you are working in HTML, the Birthday Card project makes a good starting point.

If you are working in Python, maybe you could create an ASCII art thank you card.

What To Make

Picture Animation Story Game Song Poem

Who To Thank

NHS Care workers Emergency services Cleaners Delivery drivers Public transport workers Food shop staff Postal service Gas, electric, and water workers Refuse workers Scientists

Content Ideas

Wash Your Hands Stay At Home Protect Our NHS 2 Metres Apart Vampire Cough Elbow Bump Donʼt Touch Your Face

Image Ideas

Rainbow Fireworks Sun NHS logo Heart Hands Water

Audio Ideas

Cheer Applause Whistle Rattle Drum Clap Gong Bell

Submission
