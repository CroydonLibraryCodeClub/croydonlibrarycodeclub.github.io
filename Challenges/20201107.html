Challenge 7/11/2020: Remembrance Sunday

What kind of project will you make to commemorate those who died in wars?

Remembrance Sunday facts

Remembrance Sunday falls on the second Sunday in November as it is the nearest Sunday to Armistice Day on the 11th of November.

Armistice Day marks the day World War One ended in 1918.

We remember those who died in wars by holding a two-minute silence and by wearing a red poppy.
