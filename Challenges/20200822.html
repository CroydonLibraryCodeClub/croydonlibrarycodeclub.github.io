Challenge 22/08/2020: Tycoon!

Make a game based around running a successful business. Give your player options and random challenges to deal with. Can they make a profit?

Getting Started

If you are making a Lemonade Stand game:

  1. show the player the weather report
  2. ask the player how many glasses of lemonade to make
  3. ask the player how many signs to make
  4. ask the player how much to charge for lemonade
  5. show the weather
  6. show the customers
  7. calculate the profit
