Challenge 28/11/2020: Charge

Think of a project related to the theme of Charge.

Some of the many meanings of charge

  1. cavalry charge
  2. charge your batteries
  3. fill a glass
  4. how much do you charge for lemonade?
  5. charge with a crime
  6. powder charge in a gun or cannon

Projects

Modify our Snowball fight project into a game where you fire a cannon ball to sink some pirates.

Remix my Lemonade game and allow the player to enter what they want to charge for the lemonade.

Make a Wanted! poster with our web project. What was the crime and what is the reward?

Can you create a charging up sound effect. See our Special Effects project for some ideas on how to make a charging up sound.
