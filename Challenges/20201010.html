Treasure trove facts

Treasure trove is coins, gold, or silver things, found hidden in the earth, for which no owner can be discovered.

In England and Scotland, all treasure trove belongs to the queen, no matter who finds it.

In July 2017 near the village of Rauceby in Lincolnshire, two detectorists found the largest trove of Roman coins ever discovered in Britain.
