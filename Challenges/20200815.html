Challenges 15/08/2020

Getting Started

There’s so much going on outside at this very moment: birds are flying, trees are swaying, new things are growing… And with the power of digital making, you won’t miss out on any of it. Let’s use cameras and coding to tell stories and capture life this week!

Animate your life with stop motion!

You’ll need a Raspberry Pi, Camera Module, and button with wires for this project.

Tell us a story using stop motion animation, where your imagination can grow with every changing frame! Follow along with Christina as she builds a project based on our Push button stop motion project guide.

Watch the world grow before you Pi!

You’ll need a Raspberry Pi and Camera Module for this project.

Another awesome thing you can do with a Raspberry Pi and Camera Module is create time-lapse animations to show how the world moves and changes over time! Follow our Time-lapse animation project guide to learn how.

Make an animation

You can use the web cam on your computer to capture sprite costumes or backgrounds in Scratch. Click on the green flag and press the space bar to see the kind of short animation you can make.

If you capture enough images and play them one after another, you can make a short stop motion animation. See inside the animation project to see how it works.
