Cthul who?

Cthulhu facts

He has featured in cartoon shows: The Simpsons, Rick and Morty, The Grim Adventures of Billy and Mandy, and South Park.

His name is pronounced Khlûl′-hloo - that's a tongue twister!

Howard Phillips (H. P.) Lovecraft created this fictional monster in 1928.

Cthulhu lives at the bottom of the Pacific Ocean.

He has an octopus-like head with a face of tentacles, large claws on his hands and feet, and long narrow wings. His body is green and scaly.
