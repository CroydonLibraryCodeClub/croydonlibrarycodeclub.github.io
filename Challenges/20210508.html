Challenge 08/05/2021: VE Day

On the 8th of May in 1945, Germany surrendered to the Allies of France, Great Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union. After nearly six years of the largest and most horrific war in history, there were celebrations in the streets in the UK and North America.

World War II Facts

  • World War II lasted six years
  • 250,000 Londoners had their homes destroyed by German bombers
  • Thousands of children were sent to the countryside
  • Rationing actually improved our nationʼs health
  • Computers were developed to break German codes

Projects

Can you break the coded messages in our Secret messages Python project?

Or make a recipe using only food rations with our Recipe HTML / CSS project. You can find information about food available during rationing from BBC bitesize.

Try modifying our Boat Race Scratch project to transport supplies to Britain by avoiding the U-boats.
