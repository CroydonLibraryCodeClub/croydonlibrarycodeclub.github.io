Siesta facts

Siesta is the Spanish word for nap.

In Spain, people take a siesta after their main lunchtime meal.

In Italy, the siesta is called a riposo which is the Italian word for a rest.

Did you know?

I once programmed a game for the Nintendo 3DS called Siesta Fiesta!
