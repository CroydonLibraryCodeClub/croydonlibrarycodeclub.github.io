We have been celebrating throughout the month with your amazing projects :)

Let’s continue the celebration during the last week of the month!

How do you celebrate your own or your family’s and friends’ birthday? Create a project and celebrate Scratch in your own way! Bored at home? Add randomness to an activity you do at home! This studio is dedicated to making your everyday life at home a little more interesting.

Here are a few more ideas to help get you started:

  • ✺ Make Scratch a birthday card
  • ✺ Animate a celebratory dance party
  • ✺ Create a Scratch inspired game

Leave a comment with a link to your project and one of the curators will be sure to take a look and add it to the studio.

Chain Reaction

A chain reaction is when one event causes a chain of events to occur afterwards. For example, a ball could roll onto the screen, bumping into another ball, causing it to roll away. Where does the ball go? Does it bump into something else? What happens next is up to you!
