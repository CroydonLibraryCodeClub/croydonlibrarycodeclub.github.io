Challenges 11/07/2020

Getting Started

You can follow or modify one of our projects or create something of your own.

Beat the Goalie

You can create a fun football game in Scratch with Mr C and his sidekick Zac. How many penalties can you score? This project is based on our Beat the goalie project guide (available in 10 languages).

Sprint

Illustration of two sprinting robots

Run, coders, run! Follow our Sprint project guide to make a Scratch game that’s a race to the finish line. Who’s gonna finish first? This project guide is available in 9 languages.

Scratch Cat goes skiing

It’s always snowing somewhere in the world! Why not go skiing with Scratch Cat using your digital making skills? Follow our free Scratch Cat goes skiing project guide to create this game. This project guide is available in 11 languages.
